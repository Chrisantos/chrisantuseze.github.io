I am a PhD student in Computer Science at RoboCog Lab in Oklahoma State University. I work in Artificial Intelligence at the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning & Robotics.

I got my Bachelor's in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO), Nigeria. And after that, I worked at Seamfix Limited, Nigeria as Software Engineer for three years..

My research focuses on leveraging state-of-the-art AI techniques to enhance the visual capabilities of robots, allowing them to perceive and understand their environment, and learn complex manipulation tasks. I am particularly intrigued by the challenges associated with integrating computer vision, machine learning, and robotics, and how these technologies can work in synergy to create robots that can adapt and learn from their surroundings.
